Brief Missionary Background

I am Itumeleng Hloshe, a faithful member of the church of Jesus Christ Of Latter Day Saints. I want to share with you my mission story. I served in Kenya from 2002 to 2004. It was a remarkable experience to meet and befriend people from my country, stake and ward. I can testify that being a missionary is a blessing if you obey your calling. In this podcast we will talk about the journey of many missionaries. We will talk about their preparation, the service and the return of their mission. We will also explore and talk about what they are doing now, how they have grown since their mission, what challenges they faced on their mission and at home, and what they need to enhance their life. Come and join me, Elder Hloshe, for a chat!
